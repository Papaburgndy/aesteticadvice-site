Round-by-Round Breakdown

Comfort
Winner: Ulike Air 10
The sapphire cooling plate is a genuine game-changer. It holds the treatment window at 55°F while flashing, which almost completely eliminates the sting of high-energy IPL. The Braun Pro 5 has no cooling — at higher intensity settings (4–5) it produces a noticeable snap. Not unbearable, but clearly more uncomfortable than Ulike.
Speed
Winner: Ulike Air 10
Ulike's continuous glide mode covers full legs in roughly 12 minutes. The Braun Pro 5 in glide mode takes around 20–22 minutes for the same area. If you're treating multiple areas in one session (legs + underarms + bikini), the time difference adds up noticeably.
Skin Sensing
Winner: Braun Pro 5
Braun's SensoAdapt reads your skin tone 80 times per second and adjusts intensity continuously — it's the fastest, most responsive sensor on the market. The Ulike Air 10's AI sensor is excellent but reads at intervals rather than continuously. For mixed skin tones or uneven complexions, Braun's approach offers a finer safety margin.
Dark Skin Compatibility
Winner: Ulike Air 10
Ulike is rated for Fitzpatrick I–V, which includes medium-dark skin tones. Braun explicitly rates the Pro 5 for Fitzpatrick I–IV only. If you have a medium-dark complexion, Ulike is the safer choice. Neither device is appropriate for Fitzpatrick VI.
Long-Term Cost
Winner: Ulike Air 10
Unlimited flashes means zero ongoing cost for Ulike. The Braun Pro 5's 400,000 flashes covers roughly 10+ years of average use — so for most people it's also effectively lifetime. But technically, if you're treating multiple people or large areas frequently, Ulike's unlimited count is the better long-term proposition.
Brand Trust & Evidence
Winner: Braun Pro 5
Braun has been making at-home IPL devices since 2013 — longer than almost any competitor. The Silk Expert platform has extensive published safety and efficacy data. Ulike is a newer brand (founded 2019) with strong user reviews and growing clinical evidence, but Braun's 13-year track record and German engineering heritage is still a meaningful advantage for skeptical buyers.
Price
Winner: Braun Pro 5
The Braun Pro 5 typically runs about $50 less than the Ulike Air 10 ($279 vs $329). Both include the main device and body attachment. The Braun includes a precision bikini head; Ulike sells this separately. Factor in the precision head and the price gap narrows further.
Results
Tie
Both devices deliver clinically meaningful permanent hair reduction — 70–85% by session 10–12. Independent user reports and clinical data show comparable outcomes. The Ulike's cooling may allow users to consistently use higher settings, which could marginally speed results, but the difference in clinical outcomes is not statistically significant.

Who Should Buy Which

Buy the Ulike Air 10 if: You have sensitive skin or low pain tolerance, you have a medium-dark skin tone (Fitzpatrick IV–V), you want to treat large areas quickly, or you want unlimited flashes with no future costs.
Buy the Braun Silk Expert Pro 5 if: You prefer a brand with a 13-year track record and extensive clinical data, you have Fitzpatrick I–IV skin tones, or you want the fastest skin-sensing technology for maximum safety precision.

Both devices are genuinely excellent. The gap between them in terms of results is minimal — your choice mostly comes down to comfort vs. brand heritage, and whether dark skin compatibility matters to you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Ulike Air 10 better than the Braun Silk Expert Pro 5?
For most users, yes — the cooling system, unlimited flashes, and wider skin tone range give it an edge. But the Braun wins on sensor speed and brand history. Both deliver equivalent hair reduction results.
Which is faster to use?
The Ulike Air 10 covers full legs in about 12 minutes in glide mode. The Braun Pro 5 takes roughly 20–22 minutes. Ulike is significantly faster for large body areas.
Does Ulike Air 10 work on darker skin?
Yes — it's rated for Fitzpatrick I–V. The Braun Pro 5 is only rated for I–IV. If you have a medium-dark complexion, Ulike is the appropriate choice.
How many sessions do I need with either device?
Most users see 70–80% reduction after 8–10 sessions (spaced 2 weeks apart). Near-permanent results are typically achieved by sessions 12–15, with maintenance sessions every 3–6 months after that.
