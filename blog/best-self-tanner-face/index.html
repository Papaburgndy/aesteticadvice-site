Application Tips for a Natural Result

  • Exfoliate 24 hours before — removes dead skin that causes patchy absorption
  • Apply a pea-sized barrier of moisturizer to eyebrows, hairline, and around nostrils — these areas absorb more product
  • Use clean fingertips in circular motions, work quickly
  • Blend down to the neck immediately — avoid demarcation lines
  • Use less than you think — face formulas are concentrated
  • Wash hands immediately after application

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you apply self tanner on your face without streaks?
Exfoliate 24 hours before. Apply a thin barrier of moisturizer to dry patches (eyebrows, hairline). Apply self tanner with clean fingertips in circular motions. Use less than you think — face formulas are concentrated. Blend immediately down the neck. The most common mistake is applying too much.
Can you use body self tanner on your face?
No — body formulas are typically higher DHA concentration and may clog pores. Face-specific formulas are lighter, often contain skincare actives (hyaluronic acid, vitamin C), and are designed for more sensitive facial skin.
How long does self tanner last on the face?
4–7 days, fading as skin cells turn over. The face exfoliates faster than the body due to more frequent washing. Re-apply every 4–5 days, or use a daily gradual formula to maintain consistent color.
