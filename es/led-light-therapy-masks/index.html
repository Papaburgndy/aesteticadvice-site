💡 How to Choose an LED Mask

🔴 Red vs Blue vs NIR

Red (630–660nm) = anti-aging, collagen. Blue (415nm) = anti-acne. Near-infrared (830nm) = deep cellular repair. Most quality masks combine red + NIR. Add blue if acne is a concern.

📊 Power Matters

Irradiance (mW/cm²) determines how long you need to use it. Medical-grade devices achieve clinical doses in 10–20 minutes. Budget devices may need 30–60 minutes for equivalent energy delivery.

👁️ Eye Safety

Always use provided goggles or keep eyes closed during treatment. Blue and near-infrared wavelengths can cause eye damage. Never use an LED mask without proper eye protection.

⏰ Desventajasistency Is Key

LED therapy requires consistent use — most clinical protocols use 3–5 sessions per week for 8–12 weeks to see significant results. Occasional use produces minimal benefit.
