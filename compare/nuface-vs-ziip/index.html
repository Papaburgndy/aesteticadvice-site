What does microcurrent actually do?
Microcurrent delivers low-level electrical current (measured in microamperes) that mimics the body's natural bioelectrical current. This stimulates ATP production in muscle cells, increasing muscle tone and firmness. Think of it as passive exercise for facial muscles — regular use maintains and gradually improves muscle tone.
How long do NuFace results last?
Initial results (visible lift after a session) last 48–72 hours. Long-term results from consistent use (5x/week for 60 days) last months but require maintenance sessions (2–3x/week) to sustain — similar to how gym results require continued exercise.
Can I use microcurrent if I have fillers or Botox?
Wait 2 weeks after Botox before using microcurrent. Fillers have no contraindication. Always consult your injector if unsure — most practitioners consider microcurrent safe alongside aesthetic treatments.
