⚖️ How We Compare Products

🧪 Ingredient Analysis

We review full ingredient lists, concentrations, and formulation quality. Marketing claims are ignored — we look at what's actually in the bottle and whether the evidence supports it.

📚 Clinical Evidence

Every claim is backed by peer-reviewed research. We cite the actual studies and explain what they showed — including limitations. No cherry-picked data or brand-sponsored research without disclosure.

💰 True Value Analysis

We calculate cost per active ingredient, cost per dose, and compare against clinical thresholds. A $200 product is only worth it if it actually outperforms a $20 equivalent — and we tell you which do.

👥 Real User Patterns

Beyond lab data, we analyze thousands of verified user reviews for patterns in results, side effects, and long-term performance. Real-world outcomes sometimes diverge from clinical studies.
