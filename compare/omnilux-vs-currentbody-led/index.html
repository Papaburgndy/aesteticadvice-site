How long until you see results from LED masks?
Most clinical protocols run 8–12 weeks of 3x weekly sessions. Initial improvements in skin texture and tone appear around weeks 4–6. Full collagen remodeling results are visible at 8–12 weeks. Results require maintenance sessions (2x/week) to sustain.
Is LED light therapy safe for eyes?
Both devices include eye protection. Always wear the included goggles or keep eyes closed during treatment. The light intensity is not strong enough to damage eyes with normal use and eye protection, but direct prolonged staring at LEDs is not advised.
Can I use LED mask with retinol?
Yes — use your LED mask before applying any skincare products. LED works on clean skin. After treatment, apply your normal routine including retinol if it's your night routine. Some practitioners suggest LED actually enhances retinol penetration and tolerability.
