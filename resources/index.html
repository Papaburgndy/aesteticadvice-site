Before You Buy an LED Mask

5 questions to ask before spending $200–$400 on an LED mask. Power density, wavelengths, FDA clearance, and more.

  • ✓ Does it have peer-reviewed clinical data?
  • ✓ Is it FDA-cleared (not just "FDA registered")?
  • ✓ Does it include both red AND near-infrared?
  • ✓ Is the fit flexible (silicone) or rigid?
  • ✓ What's the power density (mW/cm²)?

Before You Buy an IPL Device

The 5 questions that determine whether an IPL device will work — and be safe — for you specifically.

  • ✓ What is your Fitzpatrick skin type? (I–IV = safe)
  • ✓ Is your hair color dark enough? (dark brown/black only)
  • ✓ Does the device have a skin tone sensor?
  • ✓ How many flashes does it last? (300K+ is good)
  • ✓ What's the window size? (bigger = faster treatment)

Checklist

Before You Start Tretinoin

Starting tretinoin the right way makes the difference between a smooth transition and weeks of unnecessary irritation.

  • ✓ Start at 0.025% — not 0.05% or 0.1%
  • ✓ Apply to dry skin (wait 20 min after washing)
  • ✓ Use pea-sized amount for whole face
  • ✓ 2–3 nights/week for first month
  • ✓ SPF 30+ every morning — non-negotiable
