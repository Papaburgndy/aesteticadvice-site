Frequently Asked Questions

What order should I apply serums in?
Apply serums from thinnest to thickest consistency. Vitamin C serums go on first (after cleansing), then hydrating serums (hyaluronic acid), then thicker treatment serums. Finish with moisturizer to seal everything in.
Can I use vitamin C and niacinamide together?
Yes — this combination is safe and effective. The old concern about them reacting was based on outdated research. Modern stable formulations work well together and actually complement each other for brightening.
How long until I see results from a serum?
Hydration improvements appear within days. Dark spot fading and pore reduction take 4–8 weeks of consistent use. Anti-aging benefits from retinol take 3–6 months for visible collagen changes.
Should I use a serum in the morning or at night?
Antioxidant serums (vitamin C) are best in the morning to protect against daily UV and pollution damage. Retinol serums must be used at night only. Hydrating and repair serums can be used any time.
