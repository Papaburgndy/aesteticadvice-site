🌸 How to Choose a Collagen Supplement

📊 Dose Matters

Clinical studies showing skin benefits used 2.5–10g per day. Most powders provide 10–20g per serving — well within range. Capsule and gummy formats often provide only 1–3g per serving, requiring multiple doses to reach effective levels.

🔬 Hydrolysis is Everything

Collagen must be hydrolyzed (broken into peptides) to be absorbed. Look for "hydrolyzed collagen" or "collagen peptides" on labels. Molecular weight under 5 kDa is optimal. Enzymatic hydrolysis produces more uniform, smaller peptides than acid hydrolysis.

🐄 Bovine vs Marine

Bovine: Type I and III collagen, most affordable, widely studied. Marine: predominantly Type I, potentially higher bioavailability, sustainable sourcing. Both deliver skin benefits — choose based on dietary preference and budget.

⏰ Consistency Required

Clinical studies showing results required 8–12 weeks of daily use. Collagen supplementation is a long-term investment — effects are cumulative and gradual. Taking it irregularly delivers minimal benefit regardless of product quality.
