When will I see results from collagen supplements?
Clinical studies showing skin improvement typically run 8–12 weeks. Hydration improvements come first (4–6 weeks), elasticity and wrinkle reduction follow at 8–12 weeks. Results require daily consistent use — skipping days slows progress.
Do I need to take vitamin C with collagen?
Yes — vitamin C is an essential cofactor for collagen synthesis. Either choose a collagen product that includes vitamin C (like Garden of Life) or take a separate 500–1000mg vitamin C supplement alongside your collagen.
