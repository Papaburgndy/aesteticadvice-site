5 Skincare Mistakes You're Making

Avoid these common errors that damage your skin
Mistake #1

Over-Exfoliating

Exfoliating more than 2-3x per week breaks down your skin barrier, causing sensitivity, redness, and dehydration. Less is more.
Mistake #2

Skipping SPF

UV damage is the #1 cause of premature aging. Apply broad-spectrum SPF 30+ daily, even on cloudy days and indoors.
Mistake #3

Wrong Retinol %

Starting too strong causes irritation. Begin with 0.025-0.03% and increase slowly. More isn't always better.
Mistake #4

Not Double Cleansing

One cleanse isn't enough. Use an oil cleanser first, then a water-based one to remove all makeup and impurities.
Mistake #5

Mixing Wrong Actives

Retinol + vitamin C + AHAs together = irritation. Alternate actives or use them on different nights.
