How to Choose a Vitamin C Serum

Brighten skin and fade dark spots effectively
Why Vitamin C

The Benefits

Powerful antioxidant that brightens, prevents aging, fades dark spots, and boosts collagen production.
Types Matter

L-AA vs Derivatives

L-Ascorbic Acid (L-AA): Most effective but unstable. Derivatives: More stable but less powerful. Choose based on your needs.
Concentration

Strength Matters

Aim for 10-20% L-AA. Below 10% is ineffective. Above 20% can irritate. Check the pH—should be under 3.5.
How to Apply

Timing Is Key

Use AM after cleansing but before moisturizer. Always follow with SPF 30+. Vitamin C makes skin photosensitive.
Avoid Mixing

Incompatible Ingredients

Don't mix with niacinamide, retinol, or acids. Use vitamin C AM and actives PM. Space them 12 hours apart.
