Whitening Types

Hydrogen vs Carbamide

Hydrogen peroxide: Fast (3-7 days), less effective. Carbamide: Slower (2 weeks), more effective. Choose based on timeline.
Strips vs Kits

Which Format?

Strips: Convenient, less effective. LED kits: More effective, higher price. Trays: Most effective, longer treatment time.
