The Complete Hair Removal Guide: Laser, IPL, Electrolysis & At-Home Options

📚 In-Depth Guide 📅 2026-03-24 ✓ Evidence-Based

Hair removal is one of the most requested aesthetic treatments, and fortunately, technology has made safe, effective at-home options widely accessible. From laser to IPL to older methods like electrolysis and threading, each method has distinct pros, cons, and ideal use cases. This guide covers every hair removal modality with honest comparisons, safety data, and recommendations based on skin tone, hair type, body area, and budget.
