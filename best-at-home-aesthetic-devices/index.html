🔬 How We Rank At-Home Devices

📄 Clinical Evidence

We only include devices with at least one published peer-reviewed study. We prioritize randomized controlled trials (RCTs) over industry-sponsored open-label studies. Devices with FDA clearance are flagged — FDA clearance requires safety evidence that OTC products don't need to show.

💰 Cost Per Result

We evaluate ROI: the cost of achieving clinically meaningful results relative to professional treatment. A $60 microneedling pen that delivers 70% of clinic-level results beats a $500 device that delivers 75%. Ongoing costs (consumables, subscriptions) are factored in.

⚠️ Real-World Safety

Many at-home devices are safe when used correctly and dangerous when misused. We assess built-in safety features (auto shut-off, skin sensors, depth limits), evaluate the margin for user error, and flag devices that require technique training.

📊 Consistency Required

Every device on this list requires consistent use — typically 3–5x per week for 8–12 weeks before meaningful results. Devices that deliver visible results faster (NuFACE's instant lift, whitening strips) are noted. No device produces results from occasional use.
