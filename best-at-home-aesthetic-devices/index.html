🔬 How We Pick

Every device on AesteticAdvice is evaluated on four criteria. No device makes this list based on affiliate commission rates alone.

1. Clinical evidence: We prioritize devices backed by peer-reviewed research, randomized controlled trials, and FDA clearance. "Clinically inspired" claims without data are noted as such.

2. Real-world results: We cross-reference manufacturer claims with independent user data, dermatologist opinions, and third-party testing where available.

3. Cost-per-result: A $600 LED mask that delivers better results than a $300 device might still be the better value. We calculate ROI relative to professional alternatives.

4. Safety profile: Devices that present real risks (IPL on dark skin, deep microneedling without guidance) are flagged clearly. We do not omit risk information to make products look better.
